LINUS TORVALDS

a Finnish-American software engineer ,and from his quotes:...“Talk is cheap. Show me the code.”“Intelligence is the ability to avoid doing work, yet getting the work done.”“I'd argue that everybody wants to do something that matters”

About his life

1969-

    Torvalds was born in Helsinki, Finland, on 28 December 1969. He is the son of journalists Anna and Nils Torvalds
    At age 10 Torvalds began to dabble in computer programming on his grandfather’s Commodore VIC-20.
    He later bought the personal computer, Sinclair QL and modified the computer, especially the operating system. He programmed assembly language, text editor and a few games.

1991-

    He is the founder and the creator of the new operating system ‘Linux’, which was released in 1991. This Linux kernel is one of the most significant free and open source software.
    Linux became popular in the late 1990s when competitors of Microsoft began taking the upstart OS seriously.
    In 1996 Asteroid 9793 Torvalds was named after Linus Torvalds.

1997-2012

    JLinus Torvalds is married to Tove Torvalds, a karate champion.
    His children are Daniela Yolanda Torvalds, Patricia Miranda Torvalds and Celeste Amanda Torvalds
    In 2000, he was the recipient of the Lovelace Medal, which was conferred by the British Computer Society.
    In 2012, he was one of the recipients of the Millennium Technology Prize.
